Frequently Asked Questions

Are all reviews verified?

Yes! We verify purchases through receipt uploads and cross-reference with purchase data to ensure all reviews are from genuine buyers.

How do you prevent fake reviews?

We use multiple verification methods including purchase verification, user behavior analysis, and community reporting to maintain review integrity.

Can I review products I didn't buy?

We encourage reviews from purchased products only. However, you can share experiences from samples or gifts if clearly marked in your review.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes! Our platform is completely free for users. We're supported by affiliate partnerships that don't influence our review policies.
